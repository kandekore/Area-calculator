Area Calculator




Instructions:

  • Enter the length of the rectangular space in the "Length" input box.
  • Select the unit of measurement for the length from the dropdown menu.
  • Enter the width of the rectangular space in the "Width" input box.
  • Select the unit of measurement for the width from the dropdown menu.
  • Click the "Calculate Area" button to see the result.
  • The calculated area in square meters will be displayed below the button.
